Steam Games:

  1. Log into your Steam account
  2. Go to Games → Activate a Product on Steam
  3. Enter your product code
  4. Follow the installation prompts

PlayStation Games:

  1. Go to PlayStation Store
  2. Select "Redeem Codes" from the menu
  3. Enter your 12-digit code
  4. Download and install the game

While this is rare, if you encounter issues activating your key:

  1. Double-check that you're entering the key correctly (case-sensitive)
  2. Ensure you're using the correct platform account
  3. Try activating from a web browser instead of the app
  4. Check that the game is available in your region

If the problem persists, contact our support team immediately with your order number, and we'll provide a replacement key or refund.

We offer a 30-day refund policy for unused game keys. To request a refund:

  1. The game key must not have been activated
  2. Contact support within 30 days of purchase
  3. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  4. Refunds are processed to the original payment method

Once a key is activated, it cannot be refunded as per platform policies.
